Banned List

Rules

The Commander cardpool consists of all regulation-sized black- and white-bordered Magic cards publicly released by Wizards of the Coast. Cards are legal to play with as of their sets' prerelease.

The following is the official banned list for commander games. These cards are not legal without prior agreement from the other players in the game, and may steer your playgroup to avoid other, similar cards.

  • All cards that refer to the ante mechanic (full list here)
  • All cards that Wizards has removed from constructed formats (full list here)
  • All cards with the Conspiracy card type (full list here)
